2. Role Classification Framework

The following standardized role system is used across all factions:

Core Roles
Role	Description
Tank	High durability, absorbs damage, leads frontline
DPS (Melee)	Close-range damage dealer
DPS (Ranged)	Long-range damage dealer
Disruptor	Controls, distracts, or breaks enemy formations
Defensive Support	Shields, buffs, heals, or protects allies
Spell Caster	Ability-based burst or area damage
Extended Roles
Role	Description
Siege / Structure	Defensive or objective-based unit
Aerial Unit	Flying attacker with unique targeting rules
Spirit Unit	Low HP, high utility or effect-based unit
Champion Unit	High-impact unique character with strong presence
