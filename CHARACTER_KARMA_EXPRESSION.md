Objective

This document ensures that every character in the system visibly and behaviorally expresses their Karma alignment without altering gameplay stats.

Each character must communicate alignment through:

Behavior Cues (movement, reactions, stance)

Visual Indicators (color, aura, effects, silhouette)

Combat Tendencies (how attacks feel, not how strong they are)

2. Karma Alignment Framework
Alignment	Core Theme	Expression Style
Corrupt (Adharma)	Chaos, aggression, domination	Harsh movement, dark effects, erratic energy
Balanced (Dharma)	Discipline, order, control	Calm posture, stable effects, precision
Neutral (Karma Flow)	Adaptability, unpredictability	Subtle, shifting, ambiguous cues
3. Expression Rules (Global)
