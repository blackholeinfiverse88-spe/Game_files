Purpose: Defines gameplay identity and lore concept for every card currently implemented in the system.
Status: Scriptable Objects and base stats exist. Animations and advanced abilities are not yet implemented for all these characters.

Rakshasa Faction
Rakshas Patra

Basic Rakshasa foot soldier used as a frontline unit. Cheap and aggressive, designed to quickly pressure enemies and overwhelm defenses with numbers.

Rakshas Captain

Elite commander of Rakshasa forces. Higher durability and leadership presence makes him a stronger frontline attacker capable of leading pushes.

Rakshas Warrior

Heavy melee Rakshasa fighter built for sustained combat. Balanced health and damage allow this unit to remain effective in prolonged engagements.

Mahabindra

A powerful Rakshasa brute with high durability and destructive power. Designed to act as a heavy assault unit capable of breaking defenses.

Maya Danava

A Rakshasa master of illusion and deception. Conceptually represents manipulation and unpredictable battlefield control.

Rakshasa Shapeshifter

Mystical Rakshasa capable of transforming form. Designed to represent adaptable combat behavior and unpredictable attacks.

Vena The Corrupt

A fallen ruler consumed by Rakshasa influence. Represents a dangerous hero-level champion with aggressive combat tendencies.

Demon Madhu

Ancient demon warrior known for brutal strength and destructive power. Intended to serve as a high-impact offensive unit.

Andhaka

Blind demon lord driven by fury and chaos. Designed as a relentless attacker with strong battlefield presence.

Yaksha Guardian

A spirit guardian aligned with Rakshasa forces. Protects allied units and provides defensive support.

Naga Infiltrator

Stealthy serpent warrior capable of slipping through defenses and striking key targets.

Aranya Rakshi
